Who we are

Established in 2014, Arcon is a part of the Saksham Group and an independent arm of Sarjak Container Lines, dealing with the supply of containers all across the globe. We cater to the needs of global shipping and logistics stakeholders for procurement of all kinds of marine containers.

We offer 3 Container Supply models –

Container Sales
Container Sale with pre-set Buyback Option
One Way Movement of Containers (Point-of-Destination based with no lock-in period)

Our global fleet of containers is massive spanning Dry Vans, Open Tops, Flat Racks, Hard Tops, Super Racks, ISO Tanks and Reefer Containers.
